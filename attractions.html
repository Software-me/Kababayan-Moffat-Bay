Discover the Attractions at Loreine's Bay Resort Lodge

From thrilling outdoor adventures to serene relaxation, we have something for everyone.

Hiking Adventures

Explore breathtaking trails around the bay with scenic landscapes and wildlife. Perfect for nature lovers and adventurers!

Kayaking

Paddle through crystal-clear waters, taking in the beauty of the bay. Kayaking offers a peaceful way to explore the surroundings.

Scuba Diving

Discover vibrant marine life beneath the waves. Whether you're a beginner or expert, our scuba tours offer an unforgettable experience.

Whale Watching

Witness majestic whales breaching the water. Our tours bring you up close to these magnificent creatures in their natural habitat.

Fine Dining

Enjoy gourmet meals made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Dine with a stunning view of the bay to complete your experience.

Indoor Pool

Take a relaxing dip in our luxurious heated indoor pool. It's perfect for a refreshing swim, regardless of the weather outside.

Marina

Dock your boat at our fully equipped marina or rent one for a day of adventure. The marina offers easy access to the open water.

Family Playground

Let the kids run wild in our family-friendly playground. Plenty of activities to keep them entertained while you relax nearby.

Spa & Wellness

Relax and rejuvenate in our spa with a range of treatments to help you unwind and feel refreshed during your stay.
