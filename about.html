Welcome to Loreine's Bay Resort Lodge

Your luxury getaway in the heart of the San Juan Islands.

Our Story

Loreine's Bay Resort Lodge and Marina offers refined dining venues, a heated indoor pool, expansive accommodations, an opulent spa, and kid-friendly activities. Our marina facilities ensure a smooth and enjoyable boating trip, while the lodge provides a serene escape for visitors looking for adventure and relaxation.

Our Marina

Located next to the beautiful Joviedsa Island, our marina is the perfect spot to dock your boat and enjoy the natural surroundings. We provide top-notch facilities and services for boaters, ensuring a memorable maritime experience.

Fine Dining

Experience gourmet meals prepared with locally sourced ingredients, all while enjoying breathtaking views of the bay. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying a relaxing meal, our dining venues will exceed your expectations.
